Route

The Starting Line for the Criterium is mid-block on Main Street almost directly across from the Flanigan Law Office. Riders will travel south on Main, make a sharp turn onto Vandalia for a 300 yard straightaway on Vandalia, then enter “Ninian’s Narrows” where the course narrows for 80 feet in front of the statue for Edwardsville’s namesake, Ninian Edwards. Cyclists will then make a series of quick turns, first a left on Benton, a right on Ramey, a right on Coventry and then a right on St. Louis Street. From St. Louis the route veers at a 45 degree angle onto a 268-yard straightaway. With the Madison County courthouse on the left, cyclists will make the final turn back onto Main Street for a 90-yard sprint to the Finish Line.
